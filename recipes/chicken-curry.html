Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a traditional Asian dish that involves sliced chicken and various traditional herbs to create an impeccable dish

Ingredients

  • Sliced chicken
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon
  • Untreated oil

Steps

  1. Firstly, season the sliced chicken pieces with a mixture of the traditional herbs.
  2. Let this mixture sit for a period of 1-3 hours.
  3. Sear some vegetables in a pan with oil and gently place the chicken slices in the solution.
